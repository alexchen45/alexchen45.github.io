Chefless IQ Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Chefless IQ does not collect, sell, transmit, or share personal data. Probe information and app preferences stay on your device.

Information we collect

Chefless IQ does not collect personal information, analytics, advertising identifiers, account information, location data, or contact information.

Bluetooth and probe data

Chefless IQ uses Bluetooth to detect nearby compatible CQ60 wireless thermometer probe broadcasts and display temperature, battery, and signal information locally on your device.

This Bluetooth information is used only to power the live dashboard. It is not sent to our servers or shared with third parties.

Local app data

The app may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Probe names and color labels
  • Temperature unit preference, such as Fahrenheit or Celsius
  • Onboarding completion state
  • Recent temperature graph history while the app is in use

This information is stored locally for app functionality and is not transmitted to us.

Third-party services

Chefless IQ does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, or crash-reporting SDKs.

If users choose to share diagnostics with app developers through Apple’s system settings, Apple may provide crash reports through Apple developer tools. Those reports are governed by Apple’s diagnostics and privacy controls.

Children’s privacy

Chefless IQ is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If Chefless IQ changes in a way that affects privacy, this policy will be updated before or alongside the app update.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: atyc.support@gmail.com

Independent app notice

Chefless IQ is a third-party app and is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by CHEF iQ. CHEF iQ and CQ60 are referenced only to describe hardware compatibility.
