Basic troubleshooting

  • Make sure the probe is awake by removing it from the Smart Hub.
  • Make sure the probe has remaining battery.
  • Make sure Bluetooth is enabled on your iPhone.
  • Allow Bluetooth permission for Chefless IQ when prompted.
  • If the app shows no probe, keep the probe nearby and wait a few seconds.
  • If readings stop updating, return the probe to the hub, remove it again, and reopen the app.
